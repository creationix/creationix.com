About Creationix

Hi, my real name is Tim Caswell. I live in rural texas with my wife and 3 children. I've been programming for almost 25 years. I have a passion for creating new things and helping others ignite their own passion.

The word “creationix” is a contraction of “creation” and “ix” from linux and unix. Creationix is all about making things using open source modular software.

This school is my way to share this experience with the world. Thank you for your support, I will do my best to make programming a joy for you too.
